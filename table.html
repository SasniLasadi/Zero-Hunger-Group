Global Hunger Index (GHI)

As a monitoring instrument the Global Hunger Index measures worldwide hunger levels through time-based assessments of hunger aspects. The Global Hunger Index serves multiple purposes including it functions as a hunger awareness tool to measure these levels for nation states and regions and reveal locations that require additional action to end hunger.
